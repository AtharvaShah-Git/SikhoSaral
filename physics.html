Physics Study Materials

1. Mechanics - Motion

Mechanics studies motion and forces. Key concepts:

  • Distance: Total path length
  • Displacement: Shortest distance between points
  • Speed: Distance/Time
  • Velocity: Displacement/Time

Equations: v = u + at, s = ut + ½at²

Where: u = initial velocity, a = acceleration, t = time

2. Work and Energy

Energy is the capacity to do work:

  • Work = Force × Distance × cosθ
  • Kinetic Energy = ½mv²
  • Potential Energy = mgh

Conservation of Energy: Total energy remains constant

Unit: Joule (J)

3. Electricity Basics

Electricity involves charge flow:

  • Current (I) = Charge/Time
  • Voltage (V) = Work/Charge
  • Resistance (R): Ohm's Law V = IR

Circuit types:

  • Series: Same current
  • Parallel: Same voltage
